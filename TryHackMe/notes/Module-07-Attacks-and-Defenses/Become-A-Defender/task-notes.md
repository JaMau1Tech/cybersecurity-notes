# Overview

This room introduced the fundamentals of defensive security and the Blue Team mindset. It focused on understanding what defenders protect, gaining visibility into an organization's infrastructure, identifying risks, and applying layered security controls to prevent, detect, and respond to cyber threats. Through interactive exercises, I mapped a client's infrastructure and implemented defensive measures to secure it.

---

# Learning Objectives

- Understand defensive security and the Blue Team role
- Learn why visibility into an environment is critical
- Identify systems and infrastructure defenders protect
- Understand prevention, detection, mitigation, analysis, and response
- Apply layered security controls to protect an environment
- Develop a defensive security mindset

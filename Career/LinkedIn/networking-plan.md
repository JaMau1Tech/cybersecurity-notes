# LinkedIn Networking Plan

This document outlines my strategy for building a professional network within the Information Technology and Cybersecurity communities.

---

# Goals

- Build meaningful professional connections
- Learn from experienced IT and cybersecurity professionals
- Stay informed about industry trends
- Discover internship and entry-level job opportunities
- Develop a long-term professional network

# Weekly Networking Goals

## Every Week

- Send 10–15 personalized connection requests
- Engage with at least 5 posts from IT or cybersecurity professionals
- Share 1 update about my learning journey or portfolio
- Add completed projects to my LinkedIn profile when applicable
- Continue expanding my GitHub portfolio

---

# Connection Message Template

Hi [Name],

I'm currently transitioning into Information Technology and Cybersecurity through hands-on learning with TryHackMe, GitHub portfolio development, and formal training. I admire your experience and would appreciate the opportunity to connect and learn from your journey.

Thank you!

— Ja'Maurian

---

# Networking Best Practices

- Personalize every connection request
- Maintain a professional profile
- Be respectful of others' time
- Avoid asking for jobs immediately
- Focus on building genuine professional relationships
- Share progress consistently
- Celebrate certifications and project milestones
- Support others by engaging with their content

---

# Long-Term Goal

Build a professional network that supports continuous learning, career growth, mentorship, and opportunities within Information Technology and Cybersecurity.

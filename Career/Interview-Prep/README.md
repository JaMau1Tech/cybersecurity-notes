# Interview Preparation

This folder contains structured interview preparation materials for Information Technology and Cybersecurity positions.

Rather than preparing from scratch before every interview, reusable interview resources are maintained here to improve confidence, consistency, and professionalism.

---

## Workflow

Company Research

↓

Review Common Questions

↓

Review Behavioral Questions

↓

Review Technical Questions

↓

Practice STAR Stories

↓

Prepare Questions for Interviewer

↓

Complete Interview Checklist

↓

Mock Interview

↓

Interview

---

## Files

### common-questions.md

Answers to common interview questions used across Information Technology and Cybersecurity interviews.

---

### behavioral-questions.md

Behavioral interview questions answered using the STAR (Situation, Task, Action, Result) method.

---

### technical-it.md

Technical interview questions focused on Help Desk, IT Support, Desktop Support, and general Information Technology.

---

### technical-cybersecurity.md

Technical interview questions focused on Cybersecurity, Security Operations (SOC), Networking, Linux, and Security Fundamentals.

---

### star-stories.md

A collection of reusable STAR stories based on professional experience, technical projects, and hands-on learning.

---

### company-research.md

Template used to research companies before interviews.

---

### questions-to-ask.md

Professional questions to ask interviewers during the interview.

---

### interview-checklist.md

Checklist used before every interview.

---

### mock-interview.md

Complete mock interview script for practicing technical and behavioral interview questions.

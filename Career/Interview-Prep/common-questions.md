# Common Interview Questions

This document contains prepared responses to common interview questions for Information Technology and Cybersecurity positions.

---

# Tell Me About Yourself

I'm an aspiring Information Technology and Cybersecurity professional currently building a strong technical foundation through hands-on learning, structured training, and portfolio development.

Over the past several months I've been developing my skills through TryHackMe, my GitHub portfolio called Root & Repository, and the Root & Repository Cybersecurity Academy. Through these projects I've gained experience with Windows, Linux, networking, Git, GitHub, Markdown documentation, Python, JavaScript, SQL, and cybersecurity fundamentals.

In addition to my independent learning, I'm beginning the Information Technology program at Travis Technical College, where I'll continue developing practical skills through classroom instruction and hands-on labs.

My previous professional experience strengthened my communication, customer service, problem-solving, teamwork, adaptability, and attention to detail, and I'm excited to combine those skills with my growing technical knowledge as I begin my IT career.

---

# Why Do You Want to Work in IT?

I enjoy solving problems and learning how technology works. What started as curiosity turned into a commitment to building a career in Information Technology.

I enjoy troubleshooting, documenting what I learn, and continuously improving my technical skills. Through my portfolio, TryHackMe training, and upcoming education at Travis Technical College, I've discovered that I enjoy learning new technologies and helping solve technical challenges.

I'm looking forward to building a long-term career where I can continue learning while helping users and supporting business operations.

---

# Why Should We Hire You?

Although I'm beginning my professional IT career, I've demonstrated that I'm willing to invest in learning and improving my skills.

I've built a structured GitHub portfolio, completed hands-on technical labs, documented my work professionally, and committed myself to formal education. Combined with my customer service and professional experience, I believe I bring a strong work ethic, adaptability, professionalism, and a genuine desire to learn.

I'm looking for an opportunity where I can contribute while continuing to grow alongside experienced IT professionals.

---

# Why Are You Interested in This Company?

I'm interested in organizations that value learning, teamwork, professionalism, and technical growth.

From my research, your company provides opportunities to continue developing technical skills while supporting users and working alongside experienced professionals. Those qualities align with my long-term career goals, and I would be excited to contribute while continuing to learn.

---

# What Are Your Greatest Strengths?

My greatest strengths include:

- Continuous learning
- Problem-solving
- Organization
- Technical documentation
- Communication
- Adaptability
- Attention to detail

I believe these strengths have helped me succeed in previous roles and continue supporting my transition into Information Technology and Cybersecurity.

---

# What Is Your Greatest Weakness?

Early in my technical journey, I sometimes spent too much time trying to find the perfect solution before asking for guidance.

I've improved by learning to research effectively, document my work, and ask thoughtful questions when appropriate. That approach has helped me learn faster while continuing to build my technical skills.

---

# Where Do You See Yourself in Five Years?

In five years, I hope to have developed into a well-rounded Information Technology or Cybersecurity professional.

My goals include earning industry certifications, continuing my education, gaining real-world experience, contributing to meaningful projects, and eventually mentoring others who are beginning their careers.

I want to build a career centered around continuous learning, technical excellence, and helping organizations solve technology challenges.

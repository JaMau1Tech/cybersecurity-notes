# Behavioral Interview Questions

This document contains behavioral interview responses using the STAR (Situation, Task, Action, Result) method.

---

# Tell me about a time you had to solve a difficult problem.

## Situation

While working at Amazon, maintaining productivity and accuracy was essential when processing a high volume of customer packages.

## Task

I needed to ensure packages were correctly processed while adapting to changing workloads and maintaining quality standards.

## Action

I carefully verified package information, followed established procedures, communicated with team members when issues arose, and adjusted my workflow to maintain efficiency without sacrificing accuracy.

## Result

I consistently maintained productivity while supporting team goals and minimizing processing errors.

---

# Tell me about a time you worked on a team.

## Situation

Throughout my roles at Amazon, Coca-Cola, Murphy USA, and Allied Universal, teamwork was essential to daily operations.

## Task

I needed to communicate effectively with coworkers while supporting shared operational goals.

## Action

I maintained open communication, assisted teammates when workloads increased, and adapted to changing priorities while ensuring my own responsibilities were completed accurately.

## Result

The team maintained efficient operations while meeting productivity, customer service, and safety expectations.

---

# Tell me about a time you had to learn something new quickly.

## Situation

When transitioning into Information Technology and Cybersecurity, I needed to build technical knowledge across several new topics.

## Task

Learn operating systems, networking, programming fundamentals, databases, and cybersecurity concepts.

## Action

I created a structured learning plan using TryHackMe, GitHub, Root & Repository, and my Cybersecurity Academy. I reinforced everything by documenting my work and practicing through hands-on labs.

## Result

I developed a growing technical portfolio while building practical knowledge and demonstrating continuous improvement.

---

# Tell me about a time you made a mistake.

## Situation

Earlier in my technical learning, I occasionally spent too much time trying to solve problems completely on my own.

## Task

Improve my learning efficiency while continuing to understand technical concepts.

## Action

I began documenting my troubleshooting process, researching multiple resources, and asking questions after making a genuine effort to solve the problem independently.

## Result

I now learn more efficiently while producing better documentation and developing stronger troubleshooting skills.

---

# Tell me about a time you dealt with a difficult customer.

## Situation

While working as Assistant Manager at Murphy USA, I occasionally assisted customers who were frustrated with store-related issues.

## Task

Resolve concerns professionally while maintaining company policies and providing excellent customer service.

## Action

I listened carefully, remained calm, explained available solutions clearly, and worked toward an appropriate resolution while maintaining professionalism.

## Result

Most situations were resolved positively, helping maintain customer satisfaction and professionalism.

---

# Tell me about a time you showed leadership.

## Situation

As an Assistant Manager at Murphy USA, I regularly helped support daily operations and newer team members.

## Task

Assist employees while ensuring operations continued smoothly.

## Action

I answered questions, demonstrated procedures, prioritized tasks, and communicated clearly with the team.

## Result

Daily operations remained organized while supporting coworkers and maintaining customer service standards.

---

# Tell me about a goal you set for yourself.

## Situation

I wanted to transition into Information Technology and Cybersecurity.

## Task

Develop the technical knowledge and portfolio necessary for an entry-level career.

## Action

I created Root & Repository, began documenting every learning activity, completed TryHackMe rooms, developed the Cybersecurity Academy, and enrolled in Travis Technical College to continue building my technical skills.

## Result

I built a structured portfolio demonstrating my commitment to continuous learning and professional growth while preparing for opportunities in IT and cybersecurity.

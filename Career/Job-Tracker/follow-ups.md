# Recommended Timeline

## After Applying

- Wait approximately **5–7 business days** before sending a polite follow-up if you haven't received an acknowledgment.

## After an Interview

- Send a thank-you email within **24 hours**.
- If no timeline was provided, consider following up after **5–7 business days**.
- If the interviewer gave a decision date, wait until after that date before following up.

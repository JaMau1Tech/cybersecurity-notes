# Job Tracker

This folder serves as the central hub for managing my Information Technology and Cybersecurity job search.

Rather than relying on memory or scattered notes, every application, interview, networking interaction, and follow-up is documented here to keep my job search organized, consistent, and measurable.

## Files

### applications.md

Track every job application including the company, position, application date, resume used, cover letter used, application status, and notes.

---

### interview-log.md

Record interview details, questions asked, performance reflections, and lessons learned.

---

### networking-log.md

Track recruiters, hiring managers, mentors, and professional connections made through LinkedIn or networking events.

---

### follow-ups.md

Track thank-you emails, recruiter follow-ups, response dates, and next actions.

---

### company-wishlist.md

Maintain a list of companies I would like to work for and why they interest me.

---

### weekly-goals.md

Set weekly job search goals, networking goals, learning goals, and application targets.

---

### monthly-review.md

Review progress, completed applications, interviews, portfolio updates, and goals for the following month.

---

### job-search-checklist.md

Final checklist to complete before submitting any job application.
